About me.
I'm a student developer and designer double majoring in Computer Science/Statistics with a minor in Economics at the University of Toronto, and originally from Boston. My interests include machine learning, data science, UI/UX design, graphic design, and CG graphics.
I love creating projects that further human computer interaction (HCI), whether that be presenting data in insightful/easy to read conclusions or frameworks/apps that simplify life for the end user. Besides computing, I love travelling, exploring, and taking pictures (check out my photography Instagram).
